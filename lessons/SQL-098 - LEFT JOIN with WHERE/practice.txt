Lesson: SQL-098 — LEFT JOIN with WHERE
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a LEFT JOIN between customers and orders, then add WHERE orders.customer_id IS NULL to find customers with no orders.

Question 2:
Write a LEFT JOIN between customers and orders, filter WHERE status = 'Completed' OR orders.order_id IS NULL. This shows customers with completed orders OR no orders.

Question 3:
Find customers who have never placed an order, along with customers from Dhaka who have completed orders. Use LEFT JOIN + WHERE to combine both conditions.

Question 4:
Write a LEFT JOIN where the WHERE clause is placed correctly (after JOIN, not in ON clause). Observe the difference between: ON (orders.status = 'Completed') vs WHERE orders.status = 'Completed'. Write both versions.

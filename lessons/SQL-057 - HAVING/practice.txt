Lesson: SQL-057 — HAVING
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to find categories where total revenue exceeds 5000 using HAVING SUM(quantity * unit_price) > 5000.

Question 2:
Write a query to find stores with more than 4 items sold: HAVING COUNT(*) > 4. Group by store.

Question 3:
Management wants to identify high-performing store-category combinations. Write a query grouped by store and category where total revenue (SUM of quantity * unit_price) exceeds 3000. Sort by revenue descending.

Question 4:
Write a query to find categories where total quantity sold > 5 AND average unit_price > 300. Use HAVING with two conditions. Display category, total quantity, and average price.

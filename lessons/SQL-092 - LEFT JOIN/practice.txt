Lesson: SQL-092 — LEFT JOIN
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a LEFT JOIN between customers and orders. Display all customers including those with no orders (orders columns will be NULL).

Question 2:
Write a LEFT JOIN and filter to show ONLY customers who have NO orders: WHERE order_id IS NULL.

Question 3:
The marketing team wants to target inactive customers who have never placed an order. Write a LEFT JOIN query to find customers without any orders and display their name, email, and city.

Question 4:
Write a LEFT JOIN to show all customers with their most recent order date using MAX(order_date). Include customers without orders (showing NULL for the date). Group by customer_id.

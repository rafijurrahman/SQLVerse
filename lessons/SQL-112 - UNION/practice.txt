Lesson: SQL-112 — UNION
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a UNION to combine tracking_number and shipment_date from domestic_shipments and international_shipments. UNION removes duplicates.

Question 2:
Write a UNION to combine all Delivered shipments from both tables. Display tracking_number, status, and shipment_date. Note: tracking_number BD-1001 appears twice in domestic — UNION removes one.

Question 3:
The logistics dashboard needs a combined view of all shipments. Write a UNION query that selects tracking_number, weight_kg, status, and shipment_date from both domestic and international tables. Add a label column 'Domestic' / 'International'.

Question 4:
Write a UNION combining domestic, international, and returned shipments. Select tracking_number, weight_kg, status from all three tables. UNION removes duplicates — verify by checking if BD-1001 appears multiple times.

Lesson: SQL-121 — Subquery in WHERE
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a WHERE subquery to find all orders with amount above average:
WHERE total_amount > (SELECT AVG(total_amount) FROM orders).

Question 2:
Write a WHERE subquery to find all customers who have placed at least one order:
WHERE customer_id IN (SELECT customer_id FROM orders).

Question 3:
The sales team wants to find Premium customers who have also placed a Completed order.
Write: WHERE customer_type = 'Premium' AND customer_id IN (SELECT customer_id FROM orders WHERE status = 'Completed').

Question 4:
Write a WHERE subquery to find customers whose total spending (summed across all orders) exceeds 5000:
WHERE customer_id IN (SELECT customer_id FROM orders GROUP BY customer_id HAVING SUM(total_amount) > 5000).

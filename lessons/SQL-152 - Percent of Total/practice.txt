Lesson: SQL-152 — Percent of Total
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to calculate each sale as a percentage of total revenue:
ROUND(revenue / SUM(revenue) OVER () * 100, 2) AS "Pct of Total".

Question 2:
Write a query to calculate each sale as a percentage of its region's total:
ROUND(revenue / SUM(revenue) OVER (PARTITION BY region) * 100, 2) AS "Pct of Region".

Question 3:
The analytics dashboard needs contribution analysis. Display each sale with:
- Percent of category total: revenue / SUM(revenue) OVER (PARTITION BY category) * 100
- Percent of grand total: revenue / SUM(revenue) OVER () * 100
Round both to 2 decimal places.

Question 4:
Write a query showing all percentage breakdowns:
(A) Pct of category, (B) Pct of region, (C) Pct of grand total.
Display: product, category, region, revenue, and all three percentages.

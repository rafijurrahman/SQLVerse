Lesson: SQL-C028 — LEAD() vs LAG()
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write one query showing both LEAD and LAG side by side:
LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date) AS prev_revenue,
LEAD(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date) AS next_revenue.

Question 2:
Write a query to calculate growth rate using LEAD and LAG:
(revenue - LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date)) / LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date) * 100 AS growth_pct.

Question 3:
The trend analysis dashboard shows each sale with previous, current, and next values.
Display: sale_date, LAG(revenue) as "Previous", revenue as "Current", LEAD(revenue) as "Next".

Question 4:
Write a query using LAG(2) to look back 2 periods and LEAD(2) to look forward 2 periods:
LAG(revenue, 2) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date) AS two_periods_ago,
LEAD(revenue, 2) OVER (ORDER BY sale_date) AS two_periods_ahead.

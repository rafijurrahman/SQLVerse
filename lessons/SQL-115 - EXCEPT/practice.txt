Lesson: SQL-115 — EXCEPT
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
MySQL uses NOT IN to simulate EXCEPT. Find domestic_shipments that are NOT in returned_shipments: SELECT tracking_number FROM domestic_shipments WHERE tracking_number NOT IN (SELECT tracking_number FROM returned_shipments).

Question 2:
Simulate EXCEPT to find international shipments that were NOT returned: tracking numbers in international but NOT in returned. Display tracking_number, status.

Question 3:
The audit team needs to find shipments that were delivered without incident. Use EXCEPT simulation: all Delivered domestic shipments minus those that later appeared in returned_shipments.

Question 4:
Simulate EXCEPT using LEFT JOIN with NULL check: SELECT d.tracking_number FROM domestic_shipments d LEFT JOIN returned_shipments r ON d.tracking_number = r.tracking_number WHERE r.tracking_number IS NULL. Compare with NOT IN version.

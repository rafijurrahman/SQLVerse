Lesson: SQL-101 — JOIN with Aggregate Functions
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write an INNER JOIN between orders and order_items. Show order_id and SUM(quantity * unit_price) as "Order Total".

Question 2:
Write a JOIN to show order_id, COUNT(item_id) as "Item Count", SUM(quantity) as "Total Units", SUM(quantity * unit_price) as "Revenue". Group by order_id.

Question 3:
Operations needs an order summary. Join orders with order_items. For each order: show order_id, order_date, status, number of product lines, and total revenue. Sort by revenue descending.

Question 4:
Write a JOIN between customers, orders, and order_items. For each customer: show name, total orders, total product lines ordered, and total revenue. Group by customer. Sort by revenue descending.

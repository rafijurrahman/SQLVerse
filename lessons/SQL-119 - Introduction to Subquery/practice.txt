Lesson: SQL-119 — Introduction to Subquery
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a simple subquery to find customers who live in the same city as customer_id 1:
SELECT * FROM customers WHERE city = (SELECT city FROM customers WHERE customer_id = 1).

Question 2:
Write a subquery to find all orders where total_amount is above the average order amount:
WHERE total_amount > (SELECT AVG(total_amount) FROM orders).

Question 3:
The CRM team wants to find customers who have placed at least one order above 5000.
Write a subquery: WHERE customer_id IN (SELECT customer_id FROM orders WHERE total_amount > 5000).

Question 4:
Write a subquery to find the customer who placed the highest single order.
Use: WHERE total_amount = (SELECT MAX(total_amount) FROM orders).
Display customer name and order amount.

Lesson: SQL-015 — IS NULL
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to find all customers whose phone number is NULL.

Question 2:
Write a query to find all customers whose email is NULL AND whose phone is also NULL.

Question 3:
The customer support team needs to identify incomplete customer profiles. Find all customers who are missing either an email OR a phone number (or both). Display customer_id, first_name, last_name, email, and phone.

Question 4:
Write a query to find all customers who have a missing city OR a missing email. Count how many such customers exist by wrapping your query in a subquery: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM (...) AS incomplete.

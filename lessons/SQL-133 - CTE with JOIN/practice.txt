Lesson: SQL-133 — CTE with JOIN
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a CTE that aggregates sales by product, then JOIN the CTE result with the products table:
WITH sales_summary AS (SELECT product, SUM(revenue) AS total FROM sales GROUP BY product)
SELECT p.product_name, p.target_price, ss.total FROM products p JOIN sales_summary ss ON p.product_name = ss.product.

Question 3:
The analytics team wants to compare actual revenue to target price:
CTE: aggregate actual revenue per product.
JOIN: compare with products.target_price.
Show product name, target, actual, and variance (actual - target).

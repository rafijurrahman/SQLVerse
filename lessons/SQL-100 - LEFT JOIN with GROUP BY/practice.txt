Lesson: SQL-100 — LEFT JOIN with GROUP BY
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write an INNER JOIN between customers and orders, then SUM(total_amount) per customer.

Question 2:
Write a LEFT JOIN to show all customers and their total spending. Customers with no orders show 0 (use COALESCE(SUM(total_amount), 0)).

Question 3:
Finance needs a customer lifetime value report. Show customer name, city, total orders, total amount spent, and average order value. Sort by total amount descending.

Question 4:
Write a LEFT JOIN joining customers → orders → order_items. Calculate total units purchased (SUM of quantity) per customer. Include customers with 0 purchases.

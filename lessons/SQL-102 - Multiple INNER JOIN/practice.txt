Lesson: SQL-102 — Multiple INNER JOIN
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a JOIN connecting customers → orders → payments. Show customer name, order_id, and payment method.

Question 2:
Write a three-table JOIN: customers, orders, payments. Filter for Completed payment status. Show customer name, order_id, amount, and method.

Question 3:
Finance reconciliation: Join customers → orders → payments. Show customer name, order_date, order total_amount, payment amount, and payment method. Only include fully paid orders (payment status = 'Completed').

Question 4:
Write a four-table JOIN: customers → orders → order_items → products. Show customer name, product_name, quantity, unit_price, and total line value. Sort by total line value descending.

Lesson: SQL-159 — Window Functions with CTE
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a CTE to calculate revenue per store, then apply ROW_NUMBER in the outer query:
WITH store_rev AS (SELECT store, SUM(revenue) AS total FROM sales GROUP BY store)
SELECT store, total, ROW_NUMBER() OVER (ORDER BY total DESC) AS rank FROM store_rev.

Question 2:
Write a CTE for monthly revenue, then apply running total window:
WITH monthly AS (SELECT MONTH(sale_date) AS month, SUM(revenue) AS monthly_rev FROM sales GROUP BY MONTH(sale_date))
SELECT month, monthly_rev, SUM(monthly_rev) OVER (ORDER BY month) AS running_total FROM monthly.

Question 3:
Analytics pipeline using CTEs with window functions:
CTE 1: category-level revenue aggregation.
CTE 2: apply RANK() OVER to rank categories.
Final SELECT: show top 2 categories with their ranks.

Question 4:
Write a three-level CTE + window function pipeline:
CTE 1: sales with profit = revenue - cost.
CTE 2: region-level profit aggregation.
CTE 3: apply RANK and running total.
Final: show all ranked regions with cumulative profit.

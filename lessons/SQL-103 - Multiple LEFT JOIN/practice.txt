Lesson: SQL-103 — Multiple LEFT JOIN
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write LEFT JOIN: customers → orders → reviews. Show all customers, their orders (if any), and reviews (if any).

Question 2:
Write a query to find customers who placed orders but have NOT written reviews. Use LEFT JOIN customers → orders, then LEFT JOIN reviews, filter WHERE reviews.review_id IS NULL.

Question 3:
The customer experience team wants a full picture: customer name, number of orders, and number of reviews. Use LEFT JOINs to include all customers even if they have neither. Group by customer.

Question 4:
Write a LEFT JOIN chain: customers → orders → reviews. Calculate average rating per customer using AVG(rating). Include customers with no reviews (AVG = NULL). Sort by average rating descending.

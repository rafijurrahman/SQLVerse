Lesson: SQL-129 — ALL
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query using ALL to find sales where revenue is greater than ALL revenues in the 'Stationery' category:
SELECT * FROM sales WHERE revenue > ALL (SELECT revenue FROM sales WHERE category = 'Stationery').

Question 2:
Find all sales where revenue is greater than ALL sales from 'Chittagong':
WHERE revenue > ALL (SELECT revenue FROM sales WHERE region = 'Chittagong').

Question 3:
The analytics team wants to find the highest-performing Electronics sales -- those exceeding every Stationery sale.
Use: WHERE category = 'Electronics' AND revenue > ALL (SELECT revenue FROM sales WHERE category = 'Stationery').

Question 4:
Write a query comparing > ANY vs > ALL:
(A) > ANY: revenue beats at least one Furniture sale.
(B) > ALL: revenue beats every Furniture sale.
Write both queries and observe how many rows each returns.

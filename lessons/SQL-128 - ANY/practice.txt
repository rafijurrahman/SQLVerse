Lesson: SQL-128 — ANY
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query using ANY to find sales where revenue is greater than ANY revenue in the 'Stationery' category:
SELECT * FROM sales WHERE revenue > ANY (SELECT revenue FROM sales WHERE category = 'Stationery').

Question 2:
Find sales records where units_sold is greater than ANY record in Sylhet:
WHERE units_sold > ANY (SELECT units_sold FROM sales WHERE region = 'Sylhet').

Question 3:
The analytics team wants to find Electronics sales that performed better than at least one Stationery sale.
Use: WHERE category = 'Electronics' AND revenue > ANY (SELECT revenue FROM sales WHERE category = 'Stationery').

Question 4:
Write a query to find products that had at least one sale higher than ANY sale in the 'Furniture' category.
Use > ANY with the Furniture subquery. Display product, region, and revenue.

Lesson: SQL-C017 — LEFT JOIN vs RIGHT JOIN
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a LEFT JOIN (all customers, matched orders) and a RIGHT JOIN (all orders, matched customers). Observe which rows are in each.

Question 2:
LEFT JOIN from customers to orders = RIGHT JOIN from orders to customers (if you swap the tables). Write both to confirm they produce the same result.

Question 3:
(A) LEFT JOIN: Find all customers regardless of orders — for customer analytics.
(B) RIGHT JOIN: Find all orders regardless of valid customer records — for order auditing.
Write both queries.

Question 4:
Write a query: can you always convert a RIGHT JOIN into a LEFT JOIN by swapping table order? Write both versions and show they produce identical results.

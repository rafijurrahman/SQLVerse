Lesson: SQL-007 — AND
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to find all active products (is_active = 1) in the Electronics category.

Question 2:
Write a query to find all active Electronics products with a price greater than 1000.

Question 3:
The store wants to promote in-stock, active Electronics products priced under 2000. Write a query to find products meeting all three conditions: is_active = 1, category = 'Electronics', price < 2000.

Question 4:
Write a query to find all active products from the brand 'Logitech' or 'Sony' that have a price above 500. Use AND to ensure products are active.

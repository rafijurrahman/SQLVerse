Lesson: SQL-087 — DELETE
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a DELETE statement to remove the student with student_id = 3 from the students table. (First remove their enrollments.)

Question 2:
Write a DELETE to remove all enrollments where grade IS NULL (ungraded enrollments from incomplete students).

Question 3:
The university is purging graduated students older than 2023. Write DELETE FROM students WHERE enrollment_status = 'Graduated'. Note: first DELETE their enrollments to avoid FK violations.

Question 4:
Write a safe DELETE workflow:
Step 1: SELECT to verify which rows will be deleted: SELECT * FROM enrollments WHERE student_id = 3.
Step 2: DELETE those enrollment rows.
Step 3: DELETE the student.
Step 4: Verify with SELECT COUNT(*).

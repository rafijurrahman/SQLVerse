Lesson: SQL-097 — INNER JOIN with WHERE
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write an INNER JOIN between customers and orders, then filter WHERE status = 'Completed'.

Question 2:
Write a JOIN filtering for orders from January 2024: WHERE order_date BETWEEN '2024-01-01' AND '2024-01-31'.

Question 3:
The sales team wants completed orders from Dhaka customers. Write: JOIN customers and orders, WHERE city = 'Dhaka' AND status = 'Completed'. Show customer name, order_id, and total_amount.

Question 4:
Write a JOIN with multiple WHERE conditions: customers in 'Dhaka' or 'Chittagong', orders with total_amount > 2000, status = 'Completed'. Sort by total_amount descending.

Lesson: SQL-126 — EXISTS
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write an EXISTS query to find customers who have placed at least one order:
SELECT * FROM customers c WHERE EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM orders o WHERE o.customer_id = c.customer_id).

Question 2:
Write an EXISTS query to find customers who have at least one Completed order:
WHERE EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM orders o WHERE o.customer_id = c.customer_id AND o.status = 'Completed').

Question 3:
The CRM team wants to identify engaged customers. Find all customers who have:
(A) At least one Completed order above 3000.
Use EXISTS: WHERE EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM orders WHERE customer_id = c.customer_id AND status = 'Completed' AND total_amount > 3000).

Question 4:
Write a query combining EXISTS with other conditions: find Premium customers who have at least one Completed order AND are from Dhaka:
WHERE customer_type = 'Premium' AND city = 'Dhaka' AND EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM orders WHERE customer_id = c.customer_id AND status = 'Completed').

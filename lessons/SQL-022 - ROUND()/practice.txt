Lesson: SQL-022 — ROUND()
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to display each item's unit_price rounded to 1 decimal place.

Question 2:
Write a query to calculate the discounted price per unit: unit_price * (1 - discount_pct/100). Round the result to 2 decimal places and label it as "Discounted Price".

Question 3:
Finance needs a rounded revenue report. For each order_id, calculate total revenue (SUM of quantity * unit_price) and round to 2 decimal places. Label it "Total Revenue". Group by order_id.

Question 4:
Write a query to calculate the net payable amount for each item: quantity * unit_price * (1 - discount_pct/100). Round to 2 decimal places. Display product_name, quantity, unit_price, discount_pct, and "Net Amount".

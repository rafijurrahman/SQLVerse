Lesson: SQL-R010 — Subquery Revision
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write four subquery types:
(A) Scalar: SELECT (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders).
(B) WHERE IN: customers with orders above 3000.
(C) EXISTS: customers with Completed orders.
(D) NOT EXISTS: customers without any orders.

Question 2:
Write a FROM subquery (derived table) to get total spending per customer, then join to customers and filter for spending > 5000.

Question 3:
CRM complete analysis:
(A) Find VIP customers and their order count (SELECT subquery).
(B) Find Premium customers who have never cancelled (NOT EXISTS).
(C) Find customers with above-average spending (WHERE + aggregate subquery).

Question 4:
Write a correlated subquery to rank customers by spending without using ROW_NUMBER():
For each customer, count how many customers have HIGHER total spending. That count + 1 = their rank.
Display customer name and rank.

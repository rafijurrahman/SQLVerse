Lesson: SQL-C004 — SUM() vs AVG()
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write two queries:
(A) SUM(quantity * unit_price) — total revenue from all items.
(B) AVG(quantity * unit_price) — average revenue per line item.

Question 2:
Write a query to calculate both SUM and AVG of unit_price per category. Use GROUP BY category.

Question 3:
Finance needs a category report. For each category, display:
- Total Revenue: SUM(quantity * unit_price)
- Average Item Value: AVG(unit_price)
Sort by total revenue descending.

Question 4:
Write a query to find categories where the total revenue (SUM) is greater than 5000 AND the average unit price (AVG) is greater than 500. Use HAVING with both conditions.

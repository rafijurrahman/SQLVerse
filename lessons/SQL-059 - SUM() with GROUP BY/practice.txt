Lesson: SQL-059 — SUM() with GROUP BY
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a query to calculate total quantity sold per category: SUM(quantity) grouped by category.

Question 2:
Write a query to calculate total revenue per store: SUM(quantity * unit_price) grouped by store.

Question 3:
Finance needs a store revenue summary. For each store, show total revenue (SUM of quantity * unit_price), sorted by revenue descending.

Question 4:
Write a query to calculate total revenue and total cost per category. Display category, total revenue, total cost, and total profit. Sort by total profit descending.

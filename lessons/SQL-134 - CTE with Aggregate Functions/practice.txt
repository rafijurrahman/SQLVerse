Lesson: SQL-134 — CTE with Aggregate Functions
================================

Practice Questions

Question 1:
Write a CTE that calculates total revenue, total cost, and total profit per region:
WITH region_agg AS (SELECT region, SUM(revenue) AS total_rev, SUM(cost) AS total_cost FROM sales GROUP BY region)
SELECT *, total_rev - total_cost AS profit FROM region_agg.

Question 2:
Write a CTE to find the category with the highest average revenue:
WITH cat_avg AS (SELECT category, AVG(revenue) AS avg_rev FROM sales GROUP BY category)
SELECT * FROM cat_avg WHERE avg_rev = (SELECT MAX(avg_rev) FROM cat_avg).

Question 3:
Management needs a KPI dashboard. Write a CTE with all key aggregates:
total sales count, total revenue, total cost, total profit, average revenue per sale.
Then SELECT all KPIs from the CTE.

Question 4:
Write multiple aggregate CTEs and combine:
CTE 1: revenue by region.
CTE 2: revenue by category.
CTE 3: revenue by month (MONTH(sale_date)).
SELECT all three summaries in sequence.
